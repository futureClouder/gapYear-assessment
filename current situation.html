According to ABC news, Nearly one in six graduating seniors, now indicate that due to
the coronavirus pandemic, they will likely revise their plans of attending a four-year college in the fall and take a gap year.
What’s more, 63% of graduating seniors are uncertain whether they will be able to attend their first school choice in the fall.

According to the BBC news, one in five university applicants (19%) in the UK
had changed their mind about university attendance for the 2020 academic year
– whether in terms of their preferred university or by deferring a place
in favor of a gap year. 17% of students had changed their college plans due to Covid-19.
Of those students, 16% indicated that they would take a gap year.
